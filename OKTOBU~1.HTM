How we make money

Before you go further, read this.

We don't take a retainer. The whole model is two numbers — and you can hold us to both.

5% on what you're already doing.

We take 5% of your existing baseline — the average monthly marketplace revenue you're doing right now, before we start. This covers our cost of running the engine. Nothing more.

20% on everything we grow on top.

Every rupee of marketplace revenue above your baseline, we take 20%. This is where our earnings really live — which means we only make real money when we actually grow you.

That's the whole model. 5% of what you're doing now, 20% of everything we add.

And here's the part most agencies would never offer: that 20% tapers down as you scale. The bigger we grow you, the lower your rate on the growth. We'll walk you through exactly how on the call.

What we actually do: performance marketing on Amazon, Flipkart and quick commerce. Campaign strategy, bid management, keyword and targeting optimisation, ACoS control, scaling. We run the ad engine on your existing listings.

We don't redo your listings, create content, or build your brand store. So your listings need to already be good enough to convert. If they're not, we'll tell you honestly on the call.

You pay platform ad spends directly. We never touch your money. Monthly invoicing off your Seller Central dashboard. Audited quarterly.

One honest note: you know your margins better than we ever will. If 5% of your base plus 20% of your growth doesn't leave you enough room, this format isn't for you — and that's completely fine.
