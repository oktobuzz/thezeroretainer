How we (both) make money

The whole model is built on three numbers — and you can hold us to all of them. First, we agree on a baseline: your average monthly sales across all platforms over the 12 months before we start. Everything else is calculated against that number.

Baseline
2.5%
On your baseline revenue, every month. This covers our cost of running the engine — it's not where we make real money, it keeps the lights on while we build.
Growth — up to 2×
20%
Every rupee of monthly sales above your baseline, up to the point where we've doubled your revenue.
Scale — beyond 2×
25%
Once monthly sales cross twice the baseline. Getting a brand to 2.5× or 3× is genuinely harder — and worth more.
The maths, in plain numbers
Say your baseline is ₹10 lakh/month and we grow you to ₹25 lakh:
Base fee  2.5% × ₹10L ₹25,000
Growth to 2×  20% × ₹10L ₹2,00,000
Beyond 2×  25% × ₹5L ₹1,25,000
OktoBuzz total  on a brand we've 2.5×'d ₹3.5L/mo

What we actually do: Performance marketing on Amazon, Flipkart and quick commerce. Campaign strategy, bid management, keyword & targeting optimisation, ACoS control, scaling. We run the ad engine on your existing listings.

We don't redo listings, create content, or build brand stores. Your listings need to already be good enough to convert. You pay platform ad spends directly. We never touch your money.

One honest note: you know your margins better than we ever will. If our fees don't leave you enough room, this format isn't for you — and that's completely fine.
